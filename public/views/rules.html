HOW TO PLAY CODECRAFT
  • You can do the following:
    • Visit a workstation to do work, which improves your project quality (the green bar).
    • Speak to a coach to get project support (bestows a bonus when working on your project).
    • Speak to the Chief Elation Officer to re-energise (bestows energy level bonus).
  • Plan your day carefully: every action you take costs action points.
  • Energy will go down at the end of every cycle
  • When speaking to the CElO or a coach, press [SPACEBAR] to progress dialogue.
  • Your energy levels will be effected based on these decisions.
  • Complete your time at Codecraft without all your energy being depleted or be banished to the fourth floor permanently.
  • Your progress througout the game determines what job you end up with, the higher your energy level, the better the job offer.
