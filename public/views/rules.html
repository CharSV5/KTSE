HOW TO PLAY CODECRAFT
  • Press W, A, S and D to move.
  • Choose to visit a workstation, speak to a coach or talk to the Chief Joy Officer.
  • Plan your day carefully, each move costs 1 action point.
  • Depending on your choice, your energy could go up or down.
  • When speaking to the CJO or a coach, press space bar to see what they say next.
  • Press 'Y' or 'N' to accept the action.
  • Your energy levels will be effected based on these decisions.
  • Once you've spent all your action points, press [x] to start a new day.
  • Complete your time at Codecraft without all your energy being depleted or be banished to the fourth floor permanently.
  • Your progress througout the game determines what job you end up with, the higher your energy level, the better the job offer.
